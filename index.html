Research Areas

Conformal Prediction

Developing efficient conformal prediction algorithms with formal, distribution-free coverage guarantees for classification, regression, and graph learning tasks.

Adversarial Robustness

Creating prediction methods with provable guarantees under adversarial perturbations, using efficient single-certificate and single-sample robustness certification techniques.

Uncertainty Quantification

Quantifying and calibrating epistemic and aleatoric uncertainty in deep learning models, with applications to graph neural networks and large language models.
