In Preparation Adversarial Robustness Conformal Prediction

Efficient (Proxy) Robustness Certificates with Marginal Guarantee

Soroush H. Zargarbashi*, Mohammad Sadegh Akhondzadeh*, Aleksandar Bojchevski

In Preparation  ·  * Equal contribution

PDF

Abstract

Randomized smoothing provides state-of-the-art robustness certificates but is computationally intensive, requiring thousands of model evaluations per input to estimate certified radii with high confidence. This cost is prohibitive for large-scale deployment where certification must happen at inference time. We introduce proxy robustness certificates that approximate the true certified radius using a small number of forward passes, while maintaining a marginal coverage guarantee over the test distribution through the conformal prediction framework. By certifying not every input individually but the distribution of inputs collectively, we obtain certificates that are both efficient to compute and formally valid. Our approach bridges the gap between the theoretical guarantees of randomized smoothing and the practical constraints of real-time inference systems.
